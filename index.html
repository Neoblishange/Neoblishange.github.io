Workshop Unity

Here is my game created in a one week workshop on Unity.
It's a 2D narrative RPG game. and I used C# for this project.
In this game, we have to observe the different elements of the scene and interact with them to understand the story.
We can also speak to characters and make our own choices by choosing our preferred sentence which will have a consequence on the rest of the story.
